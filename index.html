About Us

How Our Program Works

As an aircraft technician, you have a relationship with aircraft owners. They come to you for advice and knowledge. You're their guide! Do you know aircraft owners who trust you and always ask about their airplanes? Of course, you do — we all do. They would like you to work on their airplane, but you don't have a shop. If you are an independent aircraft technician not working in a general aviation maintenance facility, and you have aircraft owners seeking your advice or customers you have done work for, consider partnering with our network to get their maintenance done throughout the year.

Join us, and let's work together to ensure that every GA aircraft owner has access to the top-notch maintenance services. Your skills and knowledge are invaluable, and we're here to help you continue making a difference.
